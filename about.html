About Me

Kelly Chapman (Kelaoke) is an art teacher by trade and has been creating and teaching art for over twenty years.

Kelly lived in London, UK, for a number of years teaching courses in printmaking, sculpture and mixed media, which sparked a real interest in working with tactile materials.

Since returning to her native Australia, Kelly has continued to teach children with profound disabilities, assisting students aged four to eighteen years in developing their creative and technical skills across multiple disciplines.

"Working with people who have multi-sensory needs and hearing/vision impairments has further developed my awareness and love for tactile materials."

These days, when Kelly is not teaching or hanging out with her beautiful family, she is playing with shapes and collages, weaving, knotting and making jewellery.

Visit Kelly's Etsy store or follow her on Facebook or Instagram.
